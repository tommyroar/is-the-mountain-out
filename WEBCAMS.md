# WEBCAMS.md - Regional Webcam Context

This document lists webcams in Washington and the surrounding region that are relevant for monitoring Mount Rainier and other geological/meteorological features.

## Primary Target: Mount Rainier

### UW Atmospheric Sciences Webcam 2
- **URL:** https://atmos.uw.edu/images/webcam2/
- **Image URL:** https://atmos.uw.edu/data/images/webcam2_latest.jpg
- **Description:** Located on the 7th floor of the ATG building at the University of Washington Seattle Campus. Pointing southeast toward Mt. Rainier.
- **Notes:** Mount Rainier is located just to the right of the rightmost tall stack on the Chemistry Building. Refreshes every 5 minutes.
- **Status:** **ACTIVE** (Switched from UW Red Square).

### UW Red Square (Legacy/Fallback)
- **URL:** https://www.washington.edu/cambots/camera1_l.jpg
- **Description:** Looking towards Mount Rainier from Red Square.
- **Status:** **TRIED** (Lower resolution or less favorable framing compared to Webcam 2).
